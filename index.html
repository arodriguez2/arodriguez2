Why Death Eaters?
As the name suggests, we Eat Death! By providing employment of course. Eating (like in fiestas) and at the same time overcoming hunger due to employment.

But why talk about unemployment?
At this time of the pandemic, we must learn our lesson that we have learned to survive. One of the biggest problems that I have observed is the unemployment of a lot of Filipinos. Having the pandemic is already burdensome and has given a lot of hardships towards the Filipinos, and the high rate of unemployment has doubled up the challenges that the Filipinos are going through.
